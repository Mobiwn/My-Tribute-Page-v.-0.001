The following list is a time line of Nietzsche's Life

  • 1844 - Friedrich Wilhelm Nietzsche is born.
  • 1854 - Enrols at the Naumburg Domgymnasium.
  • 1858 - First attempts at musical compositions.
  • 1860 - Founds literary society "Germania".
  • 1864 - Graduates from respected Schulpforta.
  • 1865 - Enrols atthe University of Leipzig.
  • 1867 - Signs up for 1 year of voluntary military servis.
  • 1868 - Meets & befriends Richard Wagner.
  • 1869 - Becomes prof. of classical philology at the Univ. of Basel. / Renounces his Prussian citizenship, remaining stateless.
  • 1872 - The Birth of Tragedy.
  • 1878 - Human, All Too Human.
  • 1883 - Thus Spoke Zarathustra.
  • 1886 - Beyond Good and Evil.
  • 1888 - Twilight of the Idols / Ecce Homo (posthumous)
  • 1889 - Suffers mental breakdown.
  • 1890 - Taken into care by his mother.
  • 1898 - Suffers stroke, leaving him partially paralyzed.
  • 1900 - Dies after having another stroke.
